About Us

The Meatery BBQ is focused on providing the highest quality barbecue possible. They offer wood fired BBQ seasoned with traditional flavors of garlic, onion, salt, pepper, along with our one of a kind spice rubs and injections. The result is high quality barbecue that is unbelievably tender and full of flavor.


Currently the Meatery BBQ partners with gyms and trainers around Columbus to offer PERFECTLY PORTIONED MEALS which help individuals eat healthy and achieve fitness goals while dealing with the fast pace of every day life.


During fall 2017 the Meatery BBQ will be releasing their full menu of smoked meats and bold flavored side dishes. These items will be delivered directly to your door and will be great for gatherings of all sizes.


Also be on the look out for Meatery BBQ spices, rubs, and sauces at select retail locations and available for purchase online!!
